HISTORY OF WEST BENGAL

West Bengal has a rich and diverse history that dates back to ancient times.
It was a significant center of trade and culture during the Maurya and Gupta empires.
The region witnessed the rise of Buddhism and Hinduism, which have profoundly influenced its social fabric.
During the medieval period, West Bengal was ruled by various dynasties, including the Palas and Senas.
The British colonial period brought significant changes, including the Bengal Renaissance, which saw a flourishing of art, literature, and social reform.
