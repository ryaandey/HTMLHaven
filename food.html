FOOD OF WEST BENGAL

West Bengal's cuisine is renowned for its variety and flavor, reflecting the state's cultural diversity.
Rice is the staple food, often accompanied by fish, lentils, and vegetables.
The Bengali fish curry, particularly Hilsa, is a highlight, as are the famous Bengali sweets, such as Rosogolla and Sandesh.
Mustard oil and spices like cumin and coriander are commonly used, adding depth to the dishes.
Festivals often feature elaborate meals, showcasing the importance of food in social and cultural gatherings.
