CULTURE OF WEST BENGAL

The culture of West Bengal is a vibrant blend of traditions, art, and literature.
The state is famous for its contributions to Bengali literature, with renowned poets and authors like Rabindranath Tagore and Kazi Nazrul Islam hailing from the region.
Festivals such as Durga Puja and Poila Baisakh are celebrated with great fervor, showcasing the rich cultural heritage.
West Bengal is also known for its classical music and dance forms, including Baul, Rabindra Sangeet, and Kathak.
The arts and crafts, particularly terracotta, weaving, and clay pottery, reflect the creativity and skill of its artisans.
