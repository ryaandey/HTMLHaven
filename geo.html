GEOGRAPHY OF WEST BENGAL

West Bengal is located in eastern India, bordered by Bangladesh to the east, the Himalayas to the north, and the Bay of Bengal to the south.
The state features a diverse landscape, including the lush green plains of the Ganges delta, the hilly terrains of Darjeeling, and the Sundarbans mangrove forests, which are home to the Royal Bengal Tiger.
The region experiences a subtropical climate, with heavy monsoon rains contributing to its fertile agriculture.
